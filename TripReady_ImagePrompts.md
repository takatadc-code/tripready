## 1. アプリアイコン（App Store用 1024×1024）

### ChatGPT (DALL-E 3) 用

```
Design a modern, minimalist mobile app icon for a travel management app called "TripReady". The icon should be exactly square (1024x1024), with NO text or letters on it.

Concept: A stylized suitcase or travel bag combined with a checkmark symbol, representing "travel preparation complete".

Style guidelines:
- Clean, flat design with subtle gradients (no photorealism)
- Primary color: deep sky blue (#0EA5E9) transitioning to teal (#14B8A6)
- Background: white or very light gradient
- The icon must be instantly recognizable at small sizes (even 29x29 pixels)
- Rounded corners are NOT needed (Apple adds them automatically)
- No text, no letters, no words whatsoever
- Single focal object, centered
- Similar quality level to icons of popular travel apps like Flighty, TripIt, or Google Maps
- The design should convey: preparedness, organization, confidence, international travel
```

### Gemini (Imagen 3) 用

```
Generate a square app icon (1024x1024 pixels) for a travel management app. No text.

A minimalist, flat-design icon featuring a stylized suitcase with a glowing checkmark overlay. Color palette: gradient from deep sky blue (#0EA5E9) to teal (#14B8A6). White or light background. Clean vector-art style. The icon must read clearly at very small sizes. No text, no letters. Professional, modern, premium feel. Single centered object.
```

## プロンプト使用時のTips

1. **ChatGPT (DALL-E 3)** は日本語テキストの描画が苦手なので、テキスト部分は後からFigmaやCanvaで重ねるのがベスト
2. **Gemini (Imagen 3)** は短く的確なプロンプトの方が良い結果が出やすい
3. アイコンは必ず複数パターン生成して、小サイズ（29px, 60px）での視認性をテストすること
4. App Storeスクリーンショットは実際のアプリ画面をはめ込む前提でフレームだけ生成
5. 気に入ったものが出たら「Keep the exact same style, but...」で微調整のバリエーションを出す
